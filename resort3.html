Miramar Beach, Destin, Florida

Located just 250 yards to the beach, Florida on 30a is located in a less crowded section of the Miramar Beach, along the bike path, and only a few minutes walk to Gulf Place Town Center. A large private pool is surrounded by nature with the pool deck offering plenty of chaise lounges and seating.

Activities

Water skiing, tennis, scuba diving, kitesurf, spa are just a few amenities that the Tides area has to offer.

Facilities

Upon entering the home, youll encounter a rustic, spacious living room with sectional sofa, leather chairs, 60 SmartTV with DVD player, wine bar, fully equipped kitchen, with plenty of dining seating at a large table and the bar area. Also featured on this floor, an oversized laundry room with extra full refrigerator, and King bedroom with pull out Twin oversize chair and 55? Smart TV.

Comfort

The first floor offers access to the pool and pool deck as well as two additional bedrooms. The Bunk Room features two bunk beds with Twins on top and Full size beds on the bottom bunk with easy access stairs for the top bunk! The other bedroom on the first floor offers Twin beds and a large flat screen TV and DVD player.

About the resort environment

Immerse yourself in the beauty of the soft white powdery beach and clear emerald green waters steps away from the condo. Our beach was recently voted the 1 Best Beach in the nation by Southern Living magazine! Daily chair rentals are offered along the resort’s 600 ft. of private beach. Relax by either of the two pools (one is even heated!) and cool off with refreshments from the Pavilion Grill & Bar or Poolside Tiki Bar. This is you, for you!
