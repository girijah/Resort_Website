Casa del Sol

-The place for fun!-

Cottage

Casa del Sol sits atop a small mountain in the peaceful gated preserve of Roma del Mar. Enjoy the spectacular scenic nature of the jungle with over 800 species of birds, playful monkeys, and beautiful flora. The weather is perfect year round with an average temperature in the mid 80s

Activities

Kids club,golf, scuba diving, flying trapeze, tennis, sailing are just a few amenities that the Casa del Sol area has to offer.

Facilities

Each well-appointed bedroom has a flat screen TV and DVD player. The bathroom suites include tiled, standalone showers, two Jacuzzi tubs, and hair dryers in each. Free wireless is available throughout the condo. A washer and dryer are also located inside the condo. Bedding consists of King (2), Queen (1), Full (4); sleeps 14.

Comfort

Gorgeous views of the Gulf of Nicoya are yours from nearly every room in the villa. Enjoy your favorite music from the surround sound system with a stereo and CD player throughout the house and pool area. Experience the breathtaking panorama across the infinity edge pool looking out across the Gulf to the mountains of the Central Valley of Costa Rica.
